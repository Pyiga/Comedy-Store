Venue History

The Main Stage at Comedy Store has been hosting top-notch comedy performances since its establishment in 1990. Over the years, it has become a beloved venue for both comedians and audiences alike, known for its vibrant atmosphere and unforgettable shows.

Venue Policies

Our venue policies ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all guests:

  • Age Restriction: 18+ (21+ for entry to bar area)
  • No outside food or drinks allowed
  • No recording or flash photography during performances

Private Events

Host your next event at the Comedy Store! Whether it's a birthday party, corporate event, or fundraiser, we offer customizable event packages to suit your needs. Contact us for more information and to book your event.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about our venue:

  • Q: Is there parking available?
  • A: Yes, we offer free parking for all guests.
  • Q: Can I bring my own food or drinks?
  • A: Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but we have a full bar and concession stand available.
  • Q: Are there seating options for individuals with disabilities?
  • A: Yes, we offer accessible seating options. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements.
